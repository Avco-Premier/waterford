Welcome to Solis

A casual spot for big cravings — serving up classic pies (yes, including BBQ chicken), plus comfort favorites like spaghetti & meatballs, along with calzones and grinders.

You’ll find us located in Lu-Mac’s Package Store, making it easy to grab dinner and go (or hang for a bit and smell pizza doing what pizza does best: tempting you).

Open every day from 11:00 AM to 8:30 PM. Call ahead for orders, and we’ll get you fed.
